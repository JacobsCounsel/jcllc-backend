Drew Jacobs, Esq.

Founder & Managing Attorney

Drew Jacobs is the founder of Jacobs Counsel LLC — a modern law firm built for founders, athletes, creators, and entrepreneurs who defy the status quo. Licensed in New York, New Jersey, and Ohio, Drew provides strategic counsel across business law, estate planning, and emerging sectors like sports and gaming law.

Known for his bold, unconventional approach, Drew helps high-performing individuals and companies scale smartly while protecting their assets. He serves as outside general counsel to businesses across technology, EV infrastructure, and behavioral health, while directing estate practices for established firms. His clients include professional athletes, entrepreneurs, and ultra-high-net-worth individuals.
